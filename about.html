Company History - Established 1997

A Vision for Malaysia

Wawasan Hosting was born in a small apartment in Petaling Jaya in August 1997. Our founder, Encik Ahmad, saw the potential of the Information Superhighway and decided that every Malaysian deserved a spot on the World Wide Web.

As Malaysia launched the Multimedia Super Corridor (MSC), Wawasan Hosting was right there, providing the servers and dial-up connectivity that powered the first wave of Malaysian dot-coms.

"Our mission is to ensure Malaysia is not left behind in the digital revolution of the 20th century!"
